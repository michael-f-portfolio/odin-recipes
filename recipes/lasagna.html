Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta which involves layer noodles, cheese and meat all on top of one another and then baked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the Cheese Layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
